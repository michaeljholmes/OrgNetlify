# Business Analyst 

## Role Summary
The role focuses on acting as a bridge between Product and the Development team to define low-level requirements and clarify any queries that arise during the development process.

**Scope & Accountability:** Owns requirement clarity and acceptance criteria quality, ensuring work handed to Development is clear, testable, and aligned to product intent.

**Level Range:** L1-L5 (Junior -> Business Analyst -> Senior -> Lead -> Principal)
## Skills
- Requirement elicitation and translation
- Collaborative analysis
- Stakeholder liaison
- Analytical mindset
 
## Responsibilities
- Refine medium-level requirements from the Product Owner into clear acceptance criteria.
- Work within the Development team to provide clarity on requirements.
- Work closely with UI/UX on workflows and designs for low-level components.
- Work closely with the Product Owner when gathering user and stakeholder requirements to develop a solid understanding of feature objectives.
- Escalate product-related queries from the Development team to Product.
- Help ensure feature quality can be measured effectively through clear acceptance criteria.

## Reports to
- Product Manager
