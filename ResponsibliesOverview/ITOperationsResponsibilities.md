### Identity & Access Management
- Operate and maintain identity services (e.g. Active Directory)
- Manage user, group, and service account lifecycle processes
- Maintain access control standards and auditability for IT systems

### Security & Hardening
- Apply security baselines and OS hardening standards across servers and services
- Maintain endpoint/server protection tooling where applicable
- Support vulnerability remediation and security incident response for IT systems

### OS Management & Patching
- Own OS-level patching, configuration management, and lifecycle planning
- Maintain golden images and baseline configurations (Windows and Linux)
- Ensure patch processes align to maintenance windows and change management

### Virtualised Infrastructure
- Operate Windows and Linux infrastructure running on virtualised platforms
- Provision, maintain, and decommission VMs and supporting services
- Ensure availability and backup/restore processes for IT-managed services

### Networking & Firewalls
- Maintain network configuration basics (DNS, routing, DHCP as applicable)
- Work with firewalls, VPNs, and network segmentation to support secure operations
- Troubleshoot connectivity issues impacting IT-managed services

### Standard IT Processes
- Own core IT processes (asset tracking, change management, access requests)
- Maintain IT documentation, runbooks, and operational procedures
- Provide support and escalation handling within defined SLAs

### Customer Hosted Infrastructure Support
- Support customer infrastructure that is hosted and operated by the organisation
- Coordinate operational tasks and incident response for customer environments
- Escalate platform-related issues to Platform Services when appropriate
