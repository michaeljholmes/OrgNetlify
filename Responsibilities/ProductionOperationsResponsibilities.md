### Operational Processes & Governance
- Define, document, and continuously improve repeatable processes for production data operations
  (e.g. customer merges, data transfers, controlled data fixes)
- Maintain runbooks, checklists, and operational controls (approvals, audit trails, scheduling)
- Ensure production data operations follow change control and risk management standards

### Tooling Standards & Automation
- Own and maintain the shared tooling and automation that enables safe production data operations
- Improve operational reliability by reducing manual steps, introducing validation checks, and
  standardising workflows

### Cross-Team Collaboration & Escalations
- Provide a clear operational interface for support and delivery teams for data-related escalations
- Coordinate across Product Engineering, Platform Services, and IT Operations when changes span
  application, platform, or security/networking work

### Operational Quality & Continuous Improvement
- Track recurring issues in production data operations and drive improvements to prevent repeats
- Perform post-incident reviews for production data incidents and ensure actions are implemented
- Maintain documentation for procedures and common operational scenarios
