Production Operations roles focus on running data-intensive, customer-impacting activities that sit close to production systems. This includes the repeatable execution of customer merges, data transfers, and controlled production data changes, as well as investigating and resolving data issues escalated from Support.

##### Boundaries and interfaces
  - Production Operations owns repeatable operational processes and tooling for customer merges and data movements.
  - Production Operations works closely with development teams and Platform Services when changes require application, platform, or deployment work.
  - Production Operations coordinates with IT Operations when changes impact shared networking, security controls, or corporate IT systems.
