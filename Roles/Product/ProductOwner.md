# Product Owner

## Role Summary
This role is focused on maximising product value by representing the customer, managing the product backlog (prioritised features), and bridging the gap between stakeholders (business/users) and the Development team. It focuses on what to build and why, ensuring the team delivers features aligned with strategic goals.

**Scope & Accountability:** Owns backlog quality, prioritisation, and requirement readiness, ensuring the Development team always has clear, valuable, and actionable work.

## Skills
- Problem solving
- Negotiation
- Project management
- Collaboration
- Analytical mindset
- Technical knowledge
- Communication and active listening

## Responsibilities
- Translate the Product Manager's strategy into measurable objectives to ensure value is delivered to the business.
- Collate user and stakeholder feedback and turn it into functional requirements.
- Create, manage, and prioritise features in the product backlog for the Development team.
- Build and own feature requirement specification documents.
- Work with UI/UX to create software flows and design prototypes.
- Work with BA to enrich specification documents with detailed acceptance criteria.
- Communicate feature requirements, value, and objectives to the Development team.
- Act as an escalation point for BA on in-flight, product-related questions.
- Work with Development teams to ensure delivered features meet business requirements through regular show-and-tells.
- Liaise with Development Team Leads to ensure objectives are met and communicate blockers/challenges to stakeholders.
- Work with Product and Technical teams to sign off features and plan and execute feature rollout.

## Reports to
- Head of Product
