# Team Lead

## Role Summary
The Software Team Lead is responsible for both people and technical leadership within a software engineering team. They ensure delivery of high-quality software, support the growth and performance of team members, and act as the primary coordination point between engineering, operations and product.

**Scope & Accountability:** Owns team delivery outcomes, people leadership, and coordination across Product, Design, and Operations, while maintaining engineering quality standards.

## Responsibilities
- Own the planning and delivery of work within the engineering team.
- Coordinate requirement production and refinement with the Product team, ensuring engineering input is captured and requirements are clear and feasible.
- Ensure all engineers participate in refinement activities where needed (e.g., reviewing requirements, carrying out spikes, and validating technical approaches).
- Once requirements are ready, coordinate with the appropriate team members to break down work into well-defined, workable units.
- Ensure the team adheres to coding standards, architectural guidelines, and quality practices.
- Collaborate with other teams to manage dependencies and cross-team initiatives.

## Skills
- Demonstrated experience leading projects or small teams.
- Experience in coaching, mentoring, or people management.
- Strong planning and delivery management skills (prioritisation, estimation, dependency/risk management).
- Strong stakeholder communication skills across Product, UI/UX, Development, and Operations.
- Ability to facilitate decisions, resolve conflicts, and unblock team progress.
- Strong technical judgement across architecture, quality standards, and testing strategy.
- Ability to balance short-term delivery needs with long-term maintainability and technical debt reduction.
- Incident and problem-management mindset, including root-cause analysis and preventative improvement actions.

## Reports to
- Head of Development
