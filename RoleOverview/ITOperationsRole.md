IT Operations roles are responsible for the organisation's corporate IT and
operational technology foundations.

They ensure core internal services are available, secure, and maintained through
standard IT processes (access requests, change control, incident handling, and
documentation).

### Team scope (high level)

- Identity and access foundations
- Security and hardening for IT-managed systems
- OS lifecycle management and patching
- Virtualised server infrastructure (Windows and Linux)
- Core networking needed for IT services (e.g. firewalls, VPN, segmentation)
- Supporting customer infrastructure that we host and operate (operational support)

### Boundaries and interfaces

- IT Operations owns corporate IT systems and services.
- IT Operations is not responsible for the internal product platform used by
  engineering teams (deployment workflows, runtime platforms, and developer
  self-service tooling), which are owned by Platform Services.
- IT Operations coordinates with Platform Services and Production Operations when
  incidents or changes span shared infrastructure, networking, or security.
