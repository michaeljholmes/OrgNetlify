# Development Career Progression framework

## Management Track (M)

| Level | Typical Scope | Focus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **M3** | Function-wide ownership across multiple teams | Strategy, org capability, standards, and outcomes |
| **M2** | Multi-team or department leadership | Delivery governance, cross-team alignment, people leadership |
| **M1** | Team-level management/leadership | Day-to-day people management, allocation, quality, and coordination |

## Individual Contributor Track (L)

| Level | Typical Scope | Focus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **L5** | Cross-team architecture/technical direction | Long-term technical strategy and standards |
| **L4** | Large systems / cross-team initiatives | Leadership without direct reports, standards and design authority |
| **L3** | Complex projects within a team | Strong ownership, mentoring, and technical decision support |
| **L2** | Feature/module ownership | Reliable end-to-end delivery and continuous improvement |
| **L1** | Guided delivery of smaller work | Learning, contribution, and growth |

## Simple Usage in Role Specs

For progression-track role specs, add one line under Scope & Accountability:
`**Level Range:** L2-L5 (Engineer -> Senior -> Lead -> Principal)`

For fixed-level management or specialist roles, keep a single level line (e.g., `**Level:** M2` or `**Level:** L5`).
