Technical Operations is responsible for the organisation's operational technical capabilities that keep services running reliably and support customer-impacting operations.

Technical Operations is made up of three operational sub-teams:

- Platform Services: internal platforms and tooling that enable engineering teams to build, deploy, and operate services reliably.
- IT Operations: corporate IT foundations (identity, security, OS management, virtualised infrastructure, networking for IT services).
- Production Operations: customer-impacting production data operations (e.g. customer merges, data transfers) and data-issue support.

##### Boundaries and interfaces
  - Technical Operations focuses on reliability, operational processes, and enabling delivery; it does not own application feature development.
  - Sub-team boundaries are defined by the relevant team overviews, and teams collaborate when work spans platform, IT, security, networking, and production data operations.
