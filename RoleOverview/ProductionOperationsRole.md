Production Operations roles are responsible for operating data-heavy, customer-impacting
processes that sit close to production systems.

These roles focus on the safe and repeatable execution of customer merges, data
transfers, and production data changes, and on resolving data issues escalated from
support.

### Boundaries and interfaces

- Production Operations owns repeatable operational processes and tooling for
  customer merges and data movements.
- Production Operations works closely with development teams and Platform Services
  when changes require application, platform, or deployment work.
- Production Operations coordinates with IT Operations when changes impact shared
  networking, security controls, or corporate IT systems.
