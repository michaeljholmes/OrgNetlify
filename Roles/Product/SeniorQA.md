# Senior QA

## Role Summary
The Senior QA role is responsible for leading quality practices across the product area, owning the overall test strategy, and ensuring that quality is built into delivery through strong requirements support, effective risk management, and reliable automated testing.

## Responsibilities
- Own and continuously improve the overall quality strategy for the product area (process, tooling, automation, and standards).
- Lead QA planning for releases, including defining test scope, managing risk, and providing clear quality recommendations for go/no-go decisions.
- Partner with Product/BA/UX to improve requirement quality, ensuring acceptance criteria are testable, complete, and unambiguous.
- Drive delivery of automated tests and test coverage improvements, including reducing flaky tests and improving CI feedback.
- Define and track quality metrics (escaped defects, defect trends, coverage gaps) and lead initiatives to improve them.
- Mentor and support other QAs, including reviewing test approaches, improving documentation, and sharing best practices.
- Collaborate with Engineering to improve testability, shift-left quality practices, and prevent recurring defects through root cause analysis.

## Skills
- Strong experience defining test strategy, risk-based testing approaches, and release readiness.
- Proven ability to design and maintain scalable automated test suites and improve test reliability.
- Ability to mentor other QAs and influence engineering and product teams toward better quality outcomes.
- Strong analytical and troubleshooting skills to identify root causes of quality issues and reduce defects.
- Strong communication skills for setting expectations, presenting risk, and driving decisions with stakeholders.
