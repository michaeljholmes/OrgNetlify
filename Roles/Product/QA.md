# QA

## Role Summary
The QA role is responsible for ensuring the product meets the expected quality standards across the full delivery lifecycle, supporting the Product team in developing clear requirements, and contributing to the delivery and maintenance of automated tests.

## Skills
- Strong understanding of QA principles and software development lifecycles.
- Ability to translate requirements into clear test scenarios and acceptance criteria.
- Experience with test automation frameworks and integrating automated tests into CI/CD.
- Strong attention to detail and ability to assess risk, impact, and severity.
- Clear written and verbal communication for defect reporting and stakeholder updates.

## Responsibilities
- Ensure the product meets expected quality standards by defining quality criteria and validating outcomes against them.
- Collaborate with Product/BA/UX and development to improve requirements, identify edge cases, and define acceptance criteria.
- Plan and execute appropriate testing for features and releases (risk-based, regression, exploratory, and non-functional where applicable).
- Deliver and maintain automated tests (e.g. UI, API, integration) and ensure they run reliably as part of the delivery pipeline.
- Identify, report, and triage defects with clear reproduction steps, impact assessment, and evidence, and verify fixes.
- Support release readiness by coordinating test activity, validating scope, and providing quality sign-off input.
- Use feedback and metrics (defect trends, escaped defects, flaky tests, coverage gaps) to drive continuous quality improvements.
