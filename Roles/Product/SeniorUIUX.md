# Senior UI/UX

## Role Summary
The Senior UI/UX role works closely with Product to obtain and shape requirements, rapidly create wireframes and prototypes to test ideas, and deliver high-quality designs while ensuring UI and UX standards are consistently met throughout the application.

## Skills
- Strong experience with user-centred design and translating product goals into usable flows.
- Ability to rapidly produce wireframes, prototypes, and iterate based on feedback.
- Strong visual design skills (layout, typography, colour, accessibility) and attention to detail.
- Ability to define and maintain design systems, component standards, and interaction patterns.
- Strong stakeholder communication and facilitation skills (workshops, critiques, presenting options and trade-offs).
- Ability to validate designs with users and data (testing, analytics, feedback loops).

## Responsibilities
- Partner with Product to define and refine requirements, user journeys, and success criteria for new features.
- Rapidly create wireframes and interactive prototypes to explore and validate solutions before build.
- Produce high-quality UI designs and interaction specifications that are clear and implementation-ready.
- Own and uphold UI/UX standards across the product, including consistency, accessibility, and usability.
- Maintain and evolve the design system (patterns, components, guidelines) and ensure adoption.
- Run and facilitate design reviews, providing constructive feedback and guidance to improve quality.
- Collaborate with Development to ensure designs are feasible, correctly implemented, and aligned with standards.
